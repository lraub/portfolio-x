ABOUT
We make decisions based on emotion and are engaged with brands that move us. I love working with companies and organizations to communicate their brands in memorable, captivating, and emotive ways, so that they will attract the right audiences and build lasting relationships. My expertise as a creative leader is utilizing brand strategy, expression and innovation to build leadership brands.

Specialties include: Brand Identity Development and Management, Conceptual Initiative Strategy, Consumer Packaging Design, Print and Digital Collateral, Point of Purchase Display, UI Design, User Research, User Personas, Information Architecture and Usability Testing.
